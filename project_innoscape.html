Project InnoScape

InnoScape Tech Solutions is a rapidly growing technology company that specializes in software development and innovative tech solutions. As the company expands its services and reaches a broader audience, there is a need for a comprehensive branding redesign to better reflect its cutting-edge approach and values.

Design Objectives:

● LOGO REDESIGN

The current logo lacks a modern and dynamic feel. We need a new logo that represents innovation, reliability, and the forward-thinking nature of InnoScape Tech Solutions.

⦿ Challenges

Balancing Innovation and Professionalism:

InnoScape Tech Solutions wants to be perceived as both innovative and professional. Striking the right balance in the design elements to convey this dual identity is a challenge.

Client Preference:

While the client has expressed a desire for a modern look, they also have a preference for a certain color (blue) used in their current branding. Finding a way to incorporate this preference while modernizing the overall color palette is a challenge.

● COLOR PALETTE

The existing color scheme feels outdated. So I revitalized the current color scheme with a contemporary and dynamic palette reflecting shades such as #0E131F for a deep, sophisticated base, #FEF5EF to infuse a touch of modernity and warmth, and #4D7EA8 to introduce a vibrant and tech-inspired accent. This refreshed color palette aims to resonate with the technology sector while maintaining broad appeal for corporate clients and individual consumers alike.
